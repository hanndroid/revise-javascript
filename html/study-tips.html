The Pomodoro Technique

What is it? The Pomodoro Technique is a great way to manage your time and keep focused. You separate your time into intervals, which are usually 25 minutes long. Once you have worked or studied for 25 minutes, you take a short break - usually 5 minutes long. Once you have completed 4 'Pomodoro's', you can take a longer break.

Start your countdown!

Fun Fact: The name Pomodoro comes from the Italian word for Tomato. Francesco Cirillo, who developed the method, used a Tomato shaped timer while he was a student!
